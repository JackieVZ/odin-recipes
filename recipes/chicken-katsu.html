Chicken katsu

Description

It's one of the most popular japanese dishes.

It consists of a breaded, deep-fried Chicken cutlet one to two centimeters thick and sliced into bite-sized pieces or strips, generally served with shredded cabbage and/or miso soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/2 cm thickness
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Butterfly chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken with flour, egg, and panko.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown and serve with tonkatsu sauce.

Even with deep frying, it's really an easy 3-step process!
